About buddycloud

We believe that real innovation happens when you decentralise systems and give designers and developers the power to innovate to the edge. Buddycloud's tools and services help application developers build great products using open standards, tools and services.

To build this future, we're searching for world class ops, frontend and backend developers. Drop us a line and let's change how the world builds communication software.

Simon Tennant (founder and CEO)
(email link) (linkedin) (twitter) (blog) (github)

Bio: Simon Tennant became an entrepreneur at an early stage.

He grew up in South Africa and has always been a technologist. Studying computer science at Warwick University in the UK, he left early to start his first venture.

Simon has had extensive experience building internet applications for startups and UBS, Deutsche Bank and at AOL and Linuxcare, when he launched buddycloud.

Currently he splits his time between London, San Francisco and Munich. In his spare time he enjoys long distance running, rock climbing and most activites in the mountains.

Simon helps promote open standards through his work on two internet standards committees: the XMPP Standards Foundation and the W3C federated social network incubator.

Logo

The buddycloud logo can be used in three differed ways:

Regular Logo

The regular logo version is used by defaul and always on a white background.

Monochrome Logo

The monochrome logo version is always used when the buddyclud brand color can not be usedi It is always used on a white background only.

Inverted Logo

The inverted logo version should always used when logo is placed on an area with the buddyclud brand colors.
